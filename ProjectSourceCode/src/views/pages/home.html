Note about allergens: Please be aware that due to the nature of a self-serve atmosphere, and the preparation of food items in a shared kitchen, there is a chance for cross-contact of gluten, eggs, fish, milk, peanuts, sesame, shellfish, soy, tree nuts and wheat. CU Boulder Campus Dining & Hospitality makes every effort to thoroughly train staff on the importance of avoiding cross-contact with allergenic foods and accurately labeling food allergens in each dining hall and retail food venue. This information is based entirely on information provided by ingredient manufacturers and suppliers.
